Teriyaki Chicken Rice Bowl

Description

This Teriyaki Chicken Rice Bowl is a quick, flavour-packed meal that’s perfect for busy weeknights or meal prep. Tender pieces of chicken are coated in a sticky, savoury-sweet teriyaki glaze made with soy sauce, honey, garlic, and ginger. Served over fluffy rice with your choice of steamed vegetables, it’s a balanced and satisfying dish that hits all the right notes—sweet, salty, and umami-rich with a fresh crunch from spring onions and a sprinkle of sesame seeds.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, diced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp mirin (or rice vinegar)
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger purée
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 spring onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds (optional)
  • Steamed white or brown rice
  • Steamed broccoli or stir-fried mixed veg

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, honey, mirin, garlic, ginger, and sesame oil.
  2. Add the diced chicken and let it marinate for 10–15 mins.
  3. Cook the chicken in a pan over medium heat until browned and cooked through (about 8–10 mins).
  4. While the chicken cooks, prepare rice and vegetables.
  5. Once the sauce thickens and coats the chicken, serve it over rice with veg. Garnish with spring onion and sesame seeds.
