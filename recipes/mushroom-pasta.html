Creamy Garlic Mushroom Pasta

Description

This Creamy Garlic Mushroom Pasta is the ultimate cosy comfort food. It features golden, pan-fried mushrooms simmered in a rich garlic-infused cream sauce, then tossed with perfectly cooked pasta for a silky, indulgent finish. Ready in just 20 minutes, it’s a satisfying vegetarian dish that feels restaurant-worthy yet couldn’t be easier to make at home. Sprinkle with parmesan and fresh parsley for an extra layer of flavour.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (tagliatelle or penne work well)
  • 250g mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp butter or olive oil
  • 100ml single cream
  • 30g grated parmesan (optional)
  • Salt & black pepper
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to packet instructions.
  2. Meanwhile, heat butter/oil in a pan, then add garlic and mushrooms. Sauté until mushrooms are golden.
  3. Pour in the cream and simmer for 2–3 mins until it thickens slightly.
  4. Drain the pasta and add it to the pan. Stir to coat everything in the creamy sauce.
  5. Season to taste, top with parmesan and parsley, and serve hot.
